Spire Of Dublin

A 120-meter tall stainless steel monument on O'Connell Street, the Spire symbolizes Dublin’s modern identity and stands as one of the city's most striking landmarks.

O'Conell Bridge

Connecting O’Connell Street to the south side of Dublin, this historic bridge over the River Liffey is unique for being wider than it is long.

Temple Bar

Dublin’s cultural and nightlife hub, Temple Bar is famous for its cobbled streets, lively pubs, and vibrant arts scene, attracting locals and tourists alike.
